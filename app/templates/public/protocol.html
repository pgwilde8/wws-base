Why the Burn & Lock?

Supply Scarcity (The Burn)

By burning 25% of all fees, we permanently reduce the amount of $CANDLE in existence. Simple economics: as the fleet grows and supply shrinks, value pressure moves upward for active drivers.

The "Diamond Hands" Effect

Locking tokens for 6 months prevents market dumping for gas money. This stability ensures your rewards have the best chance to mature into significant wealth by the time they unlock.

How is the "Burn" calculated?

Every network fee triggers a programmatic buy-back on the open market. 25% of that purchase is sent to a "dead address," removing it from circulation forever.

Is the 2.5% fee mandatory?

Yes. This fee funds the network's automated dispatching and the reward ecosystem. It is the standard service fee for the Green Candle Protocol.
