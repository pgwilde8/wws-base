About

Built by a Vet. Driven by Respect.

Why Green Candle Dispatch Exists

From the Flight Deck to the High Ridge

My name is Paul G Wilde, and I didn't start my career behind a desk. I started it in the U.S. Navy, serving in VA-196 as an Aviation Structural Maintenance specialist. I spent my days ensuring our aircraft were mission-ready, learning the value of precision, discipline, and hard work.

After the Navy, I traded the flight deck for the open road. I earned my CDL and spent time behind the wheel for Prime, CRST, and Panther. I've seen the long nights, the missed holidays, and the frustration of dealing with brokers who don't understand what it's like to be 80,000 lbs deep in a winter storm.

"To be honest, I couldn't handle the lifestyle for the long haul. The sacrifice you drivers make every day is immense. I transitioned into web development, but I never lost my respect for the men and women who keep this country moving."

The Mission: Trucker's Revenge

I built Green Candle Dispatch because the industry is broken. Human dispatchers take too much, brokers keep too much, and the driver is always the last one to get paid. I decided to use my skills as a software developer to build a system that works for you, not off you.

Veteran Owned

Built with the same integrity and "mission-first" mindset I learned in the Navy.

Driver Centric

I know the road. I've sat in the seat. Every feature we build is designed to make your life easier.

Transparent Tech

No hidden fees. No "broker math." Just powerful AI and a rewards system that pays you back.

Green Candle isn't just a company; it's a way for me to give back to a community I deeply admire. I'm putting the power of AI into your hands so you can earn more, save more, and finally own a piece of the network you power.

See you on the high ridge.
