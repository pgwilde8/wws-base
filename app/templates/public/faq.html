üîê The Lock Mechanics

Is the 6-month lock from my start date?

No. It is a "Rolling Lock."

Every single load starts its own individual 180-day timer.

Date Event Status
Jan 1 You Haul Load A Locked until July 1
Jan 15 You Haul Load B Locked until July 15
July 1 Load A Unlocks ‚úÖ Available to Spend

This creates a Income Stream. After your first 6 months, you start receiving unlocking funds every single week‚Äîlike a paycheck from your past self.

Why the "Burn"? Why not give me 100% back?

We give you 75% of the fee back in tokens. The other 25% is permanently destroyed ("Burned").

The Logic: Burning tokens creates scarcity. By destroying a portion of every fee, we reduce the total supply of $CANDLE. This helps protect the value of the tokens sitting in your vault.

ü§ñ The Operations

Can I drive for other dispatchers too?

Yes. We don't demand exclusivity. You are an Owner-Operator. However, you only earn $CANDLE rewards on loads processed through our system. Most drivers switch 100% to us once they see the rewards stacking up.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

The Golden Handcuffs apply. Any tokens that have already vested (unlocked) are yours to keep. Any tokens that are still locked (waiting for the 180 days) are forfeited back to the community pool. This ensures fairness to the drivers who stay committed.
