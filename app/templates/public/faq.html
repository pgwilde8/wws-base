Does your AI work with my current load board?

Yes. You provide the map (DAT, Truckstop, or TruckSmarter), and our AI "Scout" provides the driver. The Scout Extension reads the load data and sends it to our backend for Sarah or Marcus to begin outreach.

What is the 2.5% Network Fee?

When you complete a load, a 2.5% dispatch service fee is collected via our factoring bridge. We rebate 75% of that back to you as internal service credits, while the remaining 25% is burned to maintain system scarcity.

üîê How Automation Fuel Works

What is $CANDLE and why does it matter?

$CANDLE is the infrastructure unit of Green Candle Dispatch. It represents Automation Authority. While you earn $CANDLE as a reward for every load you haul, the token itself is the "fuel" that allows our AI agents to perform high-value tasks like broker negotiations and automated packet submissions for you.

How does the "Pooled Wallet" work?

All network fees flow into a Master Pooled Wallet managed as a digital clearinghouse. Your individual balance is tracked on an Internal Ledger, keeping transaction costs near zero until you are ready to use your rewards.

How do I spend my $CANDLE?

Your Available Fuel can be loaded onto a Green Candle Debit Card for fuel, food, or repairs. We use an internal service value (1 $CANDLE = $1.00) to top up your card. Or use it in-app to power AI negotiations, packet sends, and dispatch automation.

What is a "Call Pack"?

Broker phone calls have hard costs. We offer separate USD Call Packs (e.g., $99 for 300 minutes) so you never waste your automation credits on raw telecommunication fees.
