Why lock my money?

The "Diamond Hands" Effect

If we gave you the tokens immediately, most people would sell them for gas money. By locking everyone's tokens for 6 months, we prevent "dumping." This stabilizes the price, meaning your $60 has a better chance of becoming $120 or $600 by the time it unlocks.

Loyalty Reward

Dispatching is a partnership. We invest in you with our time and AI; you invest in us by holding the token. This aligns our goals: We both want the network value to go up.

Zero Friction

You don't need a crypto wallet to start. You don't need to understand blockchain. You just drive. We handle the conversion, custody, and security until the day it unlocks.

Common Questions

What happens if I quit before 6 months?

Any tokens that are currently "Locked" will be forfeited back to the network rewards pool. Any tokens that have already "Vested" (unlocked) are yours to keep forever.

Is this legal?

Yes. Technically, you are paying a 2.5% service fee for dispatch (which is standard). We simply choose to give you a 100% rebate in the form of a digital asset.
