### **Design Notes for the Truck Stop Environment:**

* **Color Palette:** Use your "Green Candle" branding prominently. High-contrast colors (e.g., vibrant green on dark grey/black) will stand out.
* **Font:** Easy-to-read, bold fonts. Avoid anything too thin or overly decorative.
* **Durability:** Consider printing on slightly thicker, perhaps even laminated, paper if you're leaving them out.
* **Visuals:** That trucker image is key. It creates instant relatability.
* **QR Code:** Make it large and clear. Truckers will scan with their phones.
* **Simplicity:** Minimal text, maximum impact. They should "get it" in 10-15 seconds.
